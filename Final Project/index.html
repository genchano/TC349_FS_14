Why Join Psi Upsilon?

Brotherhood

Whether you're looking for new friends, trying to find a social network, looking to strengthen your college experience, or searching for life long relationships, Psi Upsilon will offer you the best opportunity of doing that at MSU. The Members of Psi U are truly brothers in every sense of the word. Regularly having between 40 and 50 members, the brothers all know each other extremely well. This is a large reason why we have the best brotherhood on campus.

Leadership and Academics

The brothers of Psi U can be found as leaders on campus as well as in the classroom. Brothers have been and are currently active in The Hospitality Business Association, IFC, Student Government, The Marketing Association, Numerous Engineering Groups, and many more. The brothers of Psi Upsilon pride themselves as being the best of the best at Michigan State.

Social Network

Psi U is a great place to start or expand your social network at MSU and nationwide. The brothers regularly visit and party with other chapters all around the nation. You can meet tons of people at our Margaritaville Parties or the "Original Psi U Stop Light Party." In addition to our parties we have the privilege of mixing with the best sororities on campus. Our basement is legendary for some of the best beer pong games to go down in all of time. We have two sundecks which are awesome on hot nights. Don't believe us? Come find out for yourself.

History and Tradition

Psi U has without a doubt the best chapter house at Michigan State University. The class of the Italian-American style reflects the brothers in the house. Psi Upsilon has been on campus since 1943. We are also one of the oldest national fraternities. Our chapter house is the best place to live during your time at MSU. We have 20 bedrooms competitively priced to alternative housing options. WiFi, satellite TV and telephone service is available in every room. Our full time cook prepares meals that are infinitely better than dorm food. The house was constructed in 1926. It is well maintained and is situated on the largest residential lot in East Lansing. We are located in a historical district just a few short blocks from campus.
