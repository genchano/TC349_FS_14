Psi Upsilon Philanthropy

Psi U is proud of it's involvement within the community. Members are required to serve a minimum of 10 service hours a semester and at least two philanthropic events on campus. Psi U has been recognized by several sororities in 2014 for their participation. This includes a first place championship at Zeta Tau Alpha's event where Psi U helped raise over $1000 in just four hours! The money raised went to cancer research. Psi U also took fifth place out of twenty teams at Delta Gamma's Anchorsplash philanthropy. Psi Upsilon also took first place at the 2014 Greek Week competition. Brothers of Psi Upsilon have no trouble staying active within the community.
